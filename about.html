Who I Am

Oh, hi! Nice to meet you.

I'm an Adobe award-winning UX/UI Designer and full-stack web developer based in Baton Rouge, LA. I've spent 8+ years building things for the web - from eCommerce storefronts and brand redesigns to full-stack internal tools and AI-powered applications.

I'm currently the Website Manager at Broken Tractor, where I own everything from UX and front-end development to SEO, campaigns, and product photography across a 20,000 SKU BigCommerce catalog. Recently I shipped two solo projects: a customer-facing machine compatibility tool and a full-stack listing generator built with React, Node.js, and the Anthropic Claude API.

I work across the full stack - from wireframes and high-fidelity comps in Sketch and Adobe XD, to front-end code, to back-end APIs and automation. I'm also a public speaker and have hosted conventions and IT education events around Baton Rouge.

I'm open to full-time roles as a Web Designer, UI/UX Designer, or Front-End Developer. Reach out at michael.alford89@gmail.com.
