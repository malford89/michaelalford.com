Evergreen Working Capital

Role: Designer & UI Developer

Overview

Evergreen Working Capital was a project for Giant Leap Marketing Agency. My job was to create a business professional-looking site that had a feel of nature and growth. We leaned on the evergreen tree to update their branding and logo.

The site was an informative guide on what factoring is and how Evergreen helps you. The design direction emphasized themes of stability and organic growth to reflect the company's financial services.
