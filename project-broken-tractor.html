Broken Tractor LLC · 2022–Present

Broken Tractor eCommerce Redesign

Role: Website Manager Platform: BigCommerce (Stencil) Catalog: 20,000+ SKUs

Overview

Broken Tractor is a leading eCommerce supplier of tractor parts and equipment. When I joined in 2022, the site needed a full overhaul - from outdated page templates and poor mobile UX, to hundreds of crawl errors and slow page speeds affecting search visibility and conversions.

What I Did

I took full ownership of the BigCommerce site using the Stencil framework - designing and building scalable page templates, improving product page UX, and restructuring the overall site architecture to support a catalog of 20,000+ SKUs.

Using Lucky Orange session recordings and heatmaps, I identified specific friction points in the shopping experience and made targeted UX changes to product pages and the homepage that improved brand visibility and product findability.

Technical & SEO Work

I proactively audited and resolved site-wide errors including broken metadata, missing alt tags, duplicate content issues, and crawlability problems - reducing overall site errors by 90%. Page speed improvements were also part of this effort, directly impacting search rankings and user retention.

Email & Campaigns

I proposed and launched Cyber Monday and seasonal campaigns that contributed to double-digit year-over-year sales growth. One customer alert email I wrote and deployed generated $20,000 in revenue - the highest-performing email in company history.

Retention & Social Proof

I implemented Yotpo for customer reviews and Smile.io for a loyalty rewards program to improve retention and build social proof across the store.

Tools Used

BigCommerce Stencil Framework HTML / CSS / JavaScript Lucky Orange Google Analytics SEMrush Klaviyo Yotpo Smile.io Adobe Photoshop

Results

  • 90% reduction in site-wide errors
  • Double-digit year-over-year sales growth
  • $20,000 single email revenue - highest in company history
  • Featured in a Lucky Orange published case study for CRO and UX work
